Trip to Dubrovnik and Budva: A Journey to Remember

Traveling to Dubrovnik and Budva with friends on a school trip was an unforgettable adventure filled with history, breathtaking scenery, and endless fun. Our journey began in Dubrovnik, Croatia, where we wandered through the ancient city walls, marveled at the medieval architecture, and enjoyed panoramic views of the Adriatic Sea. Walking along the Stradun, the main street, we felt like we were stepping back in time. The highlight was taking a cable car ride to Mount Srđ, where we enjoyed stunning sunsets over the city.Read more..

Discovering Mostar: A Journey Through History and Beauty

Traveling to Mostar was like stepping into a living postcard, where history and natural beauty intertwine seamlessly. Nestled in the heart of Bosnia and Herzegovina, this enchanting city is renowned for its iconic Stari Most (Old Bridge), a stunning piece of Ottoman architecture that gracefully spans the Neretva River. Walking across this bridge, with its mesmerizing views and rich history, was a moment of awe and reflection.Read more..
